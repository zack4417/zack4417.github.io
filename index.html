Lei Zheng

PhD Student in Robotics,

The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)

I am a phd student in the Thrust of Robotics and Autonomous Systems (ROAS) under the Systems Hub at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou Campus). Before joining Ma Lab, I worked as a senior robotics engineer at XAG. The associated trajectory replanning algorithm and safe backup policy has been used in over 50 countries and regions.

My research interests include Autonomous Driving, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles, Multi-agent Systems, Motion Planning and Safe Learning-based Control.
